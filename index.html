About Me

Versatile and creative menswear designer with a passion for innovation. Proven ability to oversee the strategy and growth of product categories through an excellent understanding of global market needs and product development. Highly skilled communicator and project manager who works well in fast-paced environments with tight deadlines and within cross functional teams. Have a look for yourself by clicking on the link to my fashion design portfolio: www.megangilpin.com

Born and raised in a small town in Kansas. I attended The University of Kansas where I studied textile design and eventually transfered to The Illinois Institute of Art where I graduated with Bachaelor of Fine Arts in Fashion Design. Following graduation I moved to NYC where I have lived for the past 10 years.
